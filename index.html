Our Services

Our aim is to teach you to drive on Cape Town’s busy roads with ease and confidence. One of our main focus in driver training emphasise on learning to drive defensively, using the K53 rules in its best and easiest way possible.

PRICE:

Driving Lessons are charged at a rate of R200 per hour and a package of 10 lessons cost R1900.

Car hire on the day of the test cost R600 if the test is withing Southern Suburbs

Leaner classes cost R300 per session.

About Mother City Driving School

We offer K53 Driving lessons and Learner Classes servicing ALL areas in Cape Town especially the Southern Suburbs and East Coast.

Driving Lessons and Learner Classes are taught every day, 7 days a week. Our operating hours are from 8am till 7pm week days and from 8am till 2pm on weekends.

If you need assistance in booking your learner or driver test then we will be very happy to assist.
If there is anything you need to know then please do not hesitate to reach out because we will do our very best to help you.
